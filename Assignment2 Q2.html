Most commonly used Entities of HTML

HTML entities, also known as character entities, are special sequences of characters used to represent reserved characters or characters with special meaning in HTML. They are often used to display characters that are not directly supported or easily typed on a keyboard.

  • '& gt;' represents the greater-than symbol (>).
  • '& amp; 'represents the ampersand symbol (&).
