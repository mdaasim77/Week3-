PW (Physics Wallah)

"Physics Wallah" is a popular online platform and YouTube channel created by Alakh Pandey, an Indian educator and physics teacher. The channel primarily focuses on providing free educational content related to physics, particularly for students preparing for competitive exams such as the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) in India. Alakh Pandey, also known as "Physics Wallah," has gained a significant following by delivering physics lectures, solving problems, and explaining complex concepts in a simplified manner. His teaching style and dedication to helping students understand physics have made Physics Wallah a prominent resource for physics education in India.
